OVERVIEW

McLaren's Longtail series exists to push the boundaries of performance – they are our most extreme track-day supercars. The McLaren 600LT, fourth in the series, is no exception.

Its 3.8-litre twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers 600 PS (598 bhp) of power. The top-exit exhausts, mounted just above the driver's shoulders, channel an unforgettable soundtrack into the cabin. Unsurprisingly, the 600LT forges such a visceral connection between car and driver that it has been showered with awards.

PURE CONNECTION

The McLaren 600LT's carbon fibre MonoCell II chassis and aluminium body panels – designed to increase downforce – contribute to a dry weight as low as 1,247 kg (2,749 lbs). With an astonishing power-to-weight ratio of 481 PS (474 bhp) per tonne, the 600LT achieves a phenomenal acceleration: from 0-100 kph (0-62 mph) in just under three seconds.

Together, these elements create the heightened track-focused precision and uncompromising raw thrills that define every Longtail.
