Designed to act as the perfect introduction to the world of McLaren, the McLaren 540C blends supercar prowess with everyday practicality. Sister model to the 570S, it is fitted with a mid-mounted 3.8-litre twin turbocharged V8 engine, capable of reaching speeds of 200 kph (124 mph) in just 10.5 seconds.

With a chassis honed to create an unrivalled balance and McLaren's Brake Steer System to aid cornering, the 540C is as at home on the road as it is on the racetrack.
