OVERVIEW

Engineered with a laser focus on track performance, the McLaren 765LT packs a punch: an impressive 0-100 kph (0-62 mph) in just 2.8 seconds. A worthy successor to the Longtail name. Born from fearless engineering.

Setting a new benchmark for Longtail dynamics, the McLaren 765LT’s 4.0-litre twin-turbocharged V8 engine throws out 765 PS (755 bhp) of power and 800 Nm (590 lbft) of torque. This exhilarating drive is soundtracked by a quad-exit full-titanium exhaust system, which offers a 40 per cent weight saving over a comparable steel construction.

LIGHT FANTASTIC

The McLaren 765LT takes our relentless focus on lightness to a whole new level. As in our Formula 1™ cars, every component has had to earn its place – including the air conditioning, carpet, and audio system, which are removed as standard. Meanwhile, advanced carbon fibre technologies and body panels further reduce the car’s weight.

The result? A class-leading power-to-weight ratio of 622 PS-per-tonne at its lightest dry weight.
