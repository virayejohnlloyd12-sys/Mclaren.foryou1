OVERVIEW

PERFECTION. BY DESIGN

This is the McLaren Artura. A high-performance hybrid supercar, with meticulously engineered McLaren Carbon Fibre Lightweight Architecture and a V6 twin-turbocharged hybrid powertrain. The McLaren Artura’s Aerodynamic efficiency is more advanced than ever, expressed through pure, purposeful design.

From its ultra-lightweight engineering and formidable powertrain to the finely tuned suspension system and advanced E-differential, every element works in harmony to deliver an unwavering driving experience. This is a McLaren shaped by a singular vision: complete excellence in execution.

DEDICATION. DETERMINATION. DESIRE.

Optimised for visibility, aerodynamics, weight and ergonomics, the bodywork manages airflow for cooling, achieves an ultra-low centre of gravity and enables precise driver positioning. Advanced carbon fibre construction and Aluminium Hot Forming ensure the seamless, sculpted execution. The outcome is a masterpiece in supercar design.
