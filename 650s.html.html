OVERVIEW

The ultimate in luxury, engagement, and exhilaration – the 650S delivers unparalleled driving experiences on track and road alike. Winner of Autocar's Best Driver's Car of 2018, its design was inspired by the formidable McLaren P1.

The McLaren 650S represents five decades of Formula 1™ success transferred to the road. 0-100 kph (0-62 mph) in three seconds, a 333 kph (207 mph) top speed, and 650 PS (641 bhp) of power. This car has breathtaking performance to match its head-turning design.

DESIGNED FOR PRECISION

Not only does the McLaren 650S's styling command attention, but it was crafted to keep the driver's eyes on the road.

The dashboard is laid out simply and logically, the steering wheel uncluttered. The low cowl allows an unobstructed view, while the tops of the arches are positioned directly above the front wheels and clearly visible from the driver's seat. This enables the driver to position the McLaren 650S perfectly, whether clipping an apex at the Nürburgring or manoeuvring into a parking space.
