OVERVIEW

A supercar designed for longer journeys, the McLaren 570GT blends a dynamic drive and beautiful design with exquisite comfort.

The car's low stance and sleek, contoured nose convey both elegance and drama. The mid-mounted engine allows for a low-slung front end, reducing drag and improving visibility. Meanwhile, the panoramic roof is higher than that of the standard McLaren 570S and stretches back further to offer extra space for longer journeys.

A REFINED EXPERIENCE

Crafted to offer greater comfort on long drives, the McLaren 570GT's interior is designed around the perfect driving position. Its uncluttered architecture features a 'floating' centre console, infotainment system and sumptuous extended leather trim. The seats are, of course, heated.

The McLaren 570GT even features tyres embedded with noise-cancelling foam, for a quieter, more refined drive.
