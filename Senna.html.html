OVERVIEW

Inspired by McLaren's greatest driver and Formula 1™ icon Ayrton Senna, this road-legal track car was engineered to smash lap records. Every minute detail of the McLaren Senna™ is dedicated to enabling the driver's peak performance. Debuting in late 2017, the hand-built, limited production car sold out as soon as it was unveiled.

The McLaren Senna™ had to live up to its legendary name. Our team developed active aerodynamics – including front aero blades and a rear wing - to generate extreme levels of downforce. It was the lightest McLaren since the iconic McLaren F1, powered by our most powerful engine at the time. The resulting power-to-weight ratio is staggering: 668 PS (659 bhp) per tonne, in a ferociously responsive car that surpasses even the groundbreaking McLaren P1 GTR for pure driver engagement.

PEAK PERFORMANCE

The McLaren Senna™ was built first and foremost for the track. Its design features signature glazed doors that provide superb visibility – enabling the driver to navigate the perfect racing line.

Relentless engineering efforts led to the reassessment and re-evaluation of every component – no matter how small – in pursuit of reduced weight and maximised performance.
