UK

Our headquarters are in the UK, but we have a global network of talent. We work together across different borders to deliver our incredible products to customers in thirty international markets.

Woking - The McLaren Technology Centre was opened in 2005 by Her Majesty the Queen as the headquarters of McLaren Racing and McLaren Automotive. Built around a man-made lake in parkland just outside Woking, Surrey it houses our Engineering team, Corporate Functions and is the HQ of McLaren Racing. With an onsite restaurant, gym and leisure centre it is also home to some iconic exhibits from our historic car collection.

Woking - McLaren Production Centre sits on the same site as the McLaren Technology Centre and was opened by David Cameron in 2012. It is the facility where our logistics and manufacturing operations are housed and where all McLaren cars from the 12C to current models are produced.
